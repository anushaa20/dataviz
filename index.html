What is Electricity Mix?

The "electricity mix" refers to the combination of different energy sources used to produce electricity in a given region or country. This mix typically includes sources such as coal, natural gas, nuclear power, hydroelectric power, wind, solar, and other renewables. The specific proportions of these sources can vary widely based on factors like resource availability, technological development, environmental policies, and economic considerations
